## Background

Coastal Dent Repair does paintless dent removal and minor collision work. Like most small repair shops, their workflow lived on paper: a job sheet at intake, a whiteboard in the bay, a phone call when the car was ready. It worked until it didn't — dropped handoffs, techs pulling the wrong car, customers showing up before the job was done.

## The Problem

The shop needed visibility without complexity. Three different audiences — technicians at their workstations, management on a laptop, and a TV mounted in the waiting area — all needed a live view of the job queue. But adding logins, passwords, and account management would never get adopted by a small team that just wants to get cars done.

## What I Built

A full-stack dashboard running on Cloudflare Pages with serverless edge functions:

- **Kanban board** — four columns (To Do, In Progress, Awaiting Pickup, Completed) with drag-and-drop. Each card shows customer name, vehicle details, job type, and assigned tech.
- **Three access roles** — ADMIN (full control), USER (create and update jobs), READ_ONLY (view only). No OAuth, no email — PIN-based login fits how the team actually works.
- **TV display mode** — navigating to `/?mode=tv` auto-authenticates as a read-only display account and enters fullscreen. Built specifically for the shop's mounted Fire TV.
- **Lifecycle automation** — status transitions automatically set `startedAt`, `completedAt`, and `pickedUpAt` timestamps. There's no way to skip or fudge them.
- **Account lockout** — 5 failed PIN attempts locks the account for 15 minutes, tracked in Firestore.
- **Archive tab** — completed jobs remain queryable for throughput tracking and reference.

## Key Technical Decision

<Callout>
**PIN auth + stateless HMAC tokens instead of OAuth.**
OAuth is the right choice for consumer apps. For a five-person shop team, it's overhead nobody wants — new Google accounts, forgot-my-password flows, email verification. Instead, the server issues a stateless token (`userId.timestamp.HMAC-SHA256-signature`) on PIN login. Every mutation verifies the signature server-side without a database lookup. Simpler infrastructure, zero dependency on external identity providers, and the team was up and running in minutes.
</Callout>
